# A. 动态元件分类
## A.1. 电容

$$
i_C(t)=C \frac{\mathrm{d} u_C(t)}{\mathrm{~d} t}
$$
	
	

$$u_C(t) = \frac{1}{C}\int i_C(t)dt$$
	
## A.2. 电感
$$
u_L(t)=L \frac{\mathrm{d} i_L(t)}{\mathrm{~d} t}
$$

	
$$i_L(t) = \frac{1}{L}\int u_L(t)dt$$
