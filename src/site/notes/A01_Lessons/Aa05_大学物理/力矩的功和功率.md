# A. 力矩的功
![Pasted image 20230327082403.png|300](/img/user/Z02_ObFiles/Attachments/Pasted%20image%2020230327082403.png)


由功的定义:
$$
\begin{aligned}
d A & =\vec{F} \cdot d \vec{r}=F \sin \varphi|d \vec{r}| \\
& =\underline{F r \sin \varphi d \theta} \\
& =|\vec{r} \times \vec{F}| d \theta=M d \theta
\end{aligned}
$$
- 力矩作功的微分形式 $$\begin{aligned}
d A =M d \theta
\end{aligned}$$
- 力矩作功的积分形式
  若刚体在外力 $F$ 持读作用下，角坐标从 $\theta_1 \rightarrow \theta_2$
$$
A=\int_{\theta_1}^{\theta_2} M \mathrm{~d} \theta
$$

# B. 力矩的功率

- 力的瞬时功率 $$P=\vec{F} \cdot \vec{v}$$
- 力矩的瞬时功率 $$\quad P=\frac{d A}{d t}=\frac{M d \theta}{d t}=M \omega$$
